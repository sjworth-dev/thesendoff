Hotel Rooms vs. Private Villa

Hotel Rooms

  • Split into 4-5 rooms
  • Different floors, different schedules
  • No pool (or crowded shared pool)
  • Noise complaints from neighbors
  • No kitchen—eating out every meal
  • $200-400/night per room

Private Villa

  • Everyone under one roof
  • Common spaces to actually hang out
  • Your own private pool
  • No neighbors to disturb
  • Full kitchen for food and drinks
  • Often cheaper per person
